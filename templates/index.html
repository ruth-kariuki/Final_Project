Our Breeds

French Bulldog - Affectionately known as Frenchies, these playful and adaptable pups are known for their bat ears and flat faces.

German Shepherd - These loyal and intelligent dogs and hardworking, German Shepherds are often used as working dogs, but also make fantastic family pets.

Labrador Retriever - America's favorite dog breed for many years running, Labs are known for their friendly and outgoing personalities.

Golden Retriever - Another highly popular breed, Golden Retrievers are intelligent, eager to please, and make excellent family companions.

Poodle - Poodles come in three sizes (Standard, Miniature, and Toy) and are known for their intelligence, athleticism, and hypoallergenic coat.

Dachshund - These short-legged, long-bodied dogs come in smooth, wirehaired, and longhaired varieties. Dachshunds are known for their playful personalities and big personalities.

Bulldog - The Bulldog is a low-energy, affectionate breed known for its wrinkled face and friendly demeanor.

Beagle - Beagles are known for their distinctive howl and excellent sense of smell. Originally bred for hunting, Beagles can be stubborn but are also very loving.

German Shorthaired Pointer - This energetic breed is an excellent hunting dog, but can also be a great companion for active families.

Australian Shepherd - Originally bred for herding livestock, Australian Shepherds are intelligent, hardworking, and loyal dogs. They thrive on having a job to do and plenty of exercise.

Chihuahua - These small dogs are known for their distinctive curly coat and playful personality. Chihuahuas are also known for their small size and affectionate nature.

Pomeranian - Known for their fluffy fur and big personalities, Pomeranians are playful and loyal companions. Despite their small size, they are surprisingly athletic.

Yorkshire Terrier - Yorkies are another small breed known for their long, silky hair. They are intelligent and affectionate dogs that make great lapdogs.
